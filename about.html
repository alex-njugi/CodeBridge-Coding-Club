About the Club

We help students move from interest to practical digital skills.

CodeBridge Coding Club gives students a simple but structured entry point into coding, web design, collaboration, and project thinking.

Our Mission

Build confidence through design, coding, and teamwork.

Our mission is to create a learning space where members can explore coding in a practical and approachable way. We encourage students to ask questions, experiment, make mistakes, and improve their work over time.

  • Introduce members to web design and development basics
  • Encourage project planning and creative thinking
  • Promote teamwork, digital responsibility, and continuous improvement
What Members Gain

Skills that transfer beyond one class assignment.

Students learn how to plan a website, structure content clearly, create more attractive pages, and communicate ideas in a way that feels useful in real situations.

Club Values

Simple values that guide how we learn and work.

A

Accessibility

We aim to make content readable, clearly labeled, and easy to navigate so more users can use the site comfortably.

B

Professionalism

Even a student project can look organized, respectful, and suitable for a real client or school audience.

C

Responsible Design

We avoid exposing personal data, use clear form labels, and think about privacy when collecting user information.
