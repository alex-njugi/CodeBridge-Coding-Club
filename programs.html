Training Tracks

Our club programs are organized for clarity, balance, and steady growth.

The club schedule below shows how members move from basics to practice while keeping sessions manageable and easy to follow.

Weekly coding club program schedule
Program Main Focus Day Format

Learning Path

Members progress through a clear sequence of skills.

Beginner Track

Students start with page structure, headings, paragraphs, links, images, and semantic content organization.

Visual Design Track

Members focus on spacing, color contrast, layout alignment, tables, image styling, and page consistency.

Interactive Practice

Students build forms, image presentations, and simple dynamic elements that improve usability.

Project Readiness

We guide members on reflection writing, testing, publishing awareness, and presenting work more professionally.

Planning Matters

Every good website begins with structure and thoughtful content planning.

In our planning sessions, members think about audience, purpose, page structure, navigation, and the type of content a visitor needs first. This makes the final website look more intentional and easier to use.
